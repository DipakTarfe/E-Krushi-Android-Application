# E-Krushi-Android-Application

E-Krushi is an Android application designed specifically for farmers, providing a comprehensive digital platform to support agricultural activities. With E-Krushi, farmers can easily purchase agricultural products online, access real-time weather information, detect crop diseases, and receive expert guidance for effective disease management. This user-friendly app is tailored to meet the needs of the farming community, ensuring a seamless online shopping experience along with essential agricultural insights.

Features
Online Agricultural Marketplace: Farmers can explore and purchase a wide range of agricultural products, from seeds and fertilizers to farming tools and equipment, all in one place.

Weather Information: Stay informed with up-to-date weather forecasts to plan farming activities effectively and mitigate weather-related risks.

Crop Disease Detection: Easily identify crop diseases using built-in diagnostic tools, enhancing crop protection and health.

Disease Management Solutions: Access expert solutions and recommendations for managing and treating various crop diseases, ensuring better yield and productivity.

Technology Stack
Platform: Android
Languages: Java(for Android development)
Database: MySQL (for data management)

Purpose
E-Krushi aims to empower the farming community by providing essential resources, information, and solutions that streamline farming operations and improve agricultural outcomes.

Installation
Clone this repository.
Open the project in Android Studio.
Build and run the app on an Android emulator or physical device.
Usage
Upon installation, users can navigate through different sections:

Marketplace for browsing and purchasing products
Weather for viewing weather updates
Disease Detection for analyzing crop health
Solutions for receiving disease management advice
